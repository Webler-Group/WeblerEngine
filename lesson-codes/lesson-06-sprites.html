Lesson 6 — Sprites

Two interactive demos: Spritesheet & Sprite (atlas frame picking), and Animation & Animator with events.

Spritesheet & Sprite

Spritesheet maps a frame index to a source rect on the atlas. Sprite renders that rect via drawImage, centered at (0, 0) in local space.

Animation & Animator
