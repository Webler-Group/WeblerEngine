Click / tap a shape to inspect it. Renderer applies each node's world transform before calling draw(ctx) — subclasses draw at (0, 0) in local space.

dt Animation

Every OrbitalNode.update(dt) does this.angle += speed × dt. Multiplying by dt makes the animation frame-rate independent — it covers the same angle per second regardless of FPS.
